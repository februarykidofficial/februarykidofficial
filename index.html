PRODUCER, STUDIO ENGINEER

HIDDEN TALENT, YOUNG STAR

As a music producer I released several singles that were heard on Polish radio stations and television. Today my songs can be found on major streaming services. As a composer I created many illustrative pieces of music that were used in films and shows such as "Myszy i Szczury", "Delfinek", "Miasto Długów" etc. Recently I started producing songs for collab which are available on BeatStars.com. I am currently working as a television studio engineer, composer and music producer.
I hope my music connects people and brings joy to everyone’s life.
